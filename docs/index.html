About

I build and maintain the infrastructure that keeps schools and organizations running. Right now, I work as a Campus Technician at Everman ISD supporting a distributed K-12 network. My days jump between cabling and switch troubleshooting, imaging workstations, maintaining Active Directory, and making sure staff and students can actually teach and learn.

I am also completing a B.S. in Cloud and Network Engineering at WGU with an AWS track focus. I already hold my CompTIA A+ Core 2, and I am studying for Core 1 and Linux fundamentals now. The end goal is to move into cloud/network engineering and data center operations, with a focus on AWS and Linux.

Outside of work and school, I run a homelab and automation projects. That means an AD and Linux environment in VirtualBox, a Minecraft server with actual monitoring and backups, and whatever else I can squeeze into rack space or a spare VM. For me, “lab” does not mean toy. It means production-like practice.
